Roots to Branches

This is the digital home of Chris Baca.

It's meant to be not-cool. Not algorithm-y. Not a space where I'm trying to gain followers or build a brand.

Just a space where I'm looking at the roots of things and seeing where they lead.

To that end, you may see weird stuff or designs here; mostly it's just me experimenting with building a website from scratch. Thanks for stopping by.
